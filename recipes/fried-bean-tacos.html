Fried Bean Tacos

Home

Description

Our Fried Bean and Mushroom Tacos are a delicious veggie option that makes it easy to eat more veg in your diet and try something new!

Ingredients

  • Plain Taco Tortilla
  • Red Kidney Beans
  • Baby Gem Lettuce
  • Sliced Mushrooms
  • Chipotle Paste
  • Mayo
  • Greek Style Salad Cheese

Steps

  1. Prep vegetables
  2. Fry the vegetables
  3. Mix the Chipotle Mayo
  4. Heat up tortillas
  5. Build and serve tacos
