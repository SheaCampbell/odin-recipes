Hoisin Chicken Noodles

Home

Description

This Stir-Fried Hoisin Chicken Noodles is bursting full of flavours and makes the perfect dinner night option from HelloFresh. Cook up a fresh start!

Ingredients

  • Egg Noodle Nest
  • Diced Chicken Thigh
  • Hoisin Sauce
  • Bell Pepper
  • Spring Onion
  • Soy Sauce
  • Ginger

Steps

  1. Prep the Veg
  2. Cook the Noodles
  3. Stir-Fry the Chicken
  4. Mix and add Hoisin Glaze
  5. Boil then simmer
  6. Serve
